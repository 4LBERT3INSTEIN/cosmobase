Mission and Objectives

The core mission of COSMOBASE+ is to facilitate comprehensive updates on frontier cosmology research. We provide a platform for advanced students and researchers to engage with cutting-edge theoretical developments, innovative observational techniques, and critical numerical solutions driving the field forward.

Academic Committee

Led by a distinguished committee of national researchers from across México:

  • Universidad Autónoma de Zacatecas (UAZ)
  • Instituto de Ciencias Físicas (ICF UNAM)
  • Universidad Michoacana de San Nicolás de Hidalgo (UMSNH)
  • Facultad de Física, Universidad Veracruzana (FF UV)
